About Rustiq

Welcome to Rustiq!

We are a passionate team dedicated to bringing art to life through handmade creations. At Rustiq, we specialize in crafting beautiful handmade paintings, unique cushions, and customized designs on pottery of all shapes and sizes, including mud lamps and vases. Our mission is to add a touch of elegance and personality to your spaces with artistic pieces that reflect your style. Whether you’re looking for a bespoke design for a special occasion or a stunning piece to brighten your home, we’re here to turn your vision into reality. Explore our collection or share your ideas with us to create something truly special!
